  • Registration

  • What is the cost to get into the competition?

    It costs $12 and all of your talent and dedication to participate in the competition. Follow this link for further information.

  • Why do I have to register if I already have the Tickets?

    The registration is like a conformation of your enrollment (attendence to make it easy). It begins at 5:30PM (17:00HRS) on Friday, 11th March, i.e., right before the competition commences.

  • How to register as a Team?

    Through the ticketleap link, you have a option to sign up individually, as a Team of 5 and as a Team of 6. Make sure you enter your fellow team member's name and student numbers in the spaces provided.

    If you and your friends (less than 5) wish to stay in the same team, you will have to buy individual tickets and then send us an email at contact@innovationbattleground.com with your names, student numbers and email addresses.



    IMPORTANT :

    Your team cannot be greater than 6 Members. A Team HAS TO include 1 member from the Schulich School of Business. If you are unable to fill that or any number of positions, its OKAY! We'll appoint the remaining members to the team.



    For Schulich Students:

    You are NOT allowed to team up with your fellow Schulich students. You can only sign up individually or with fellow Lassonde Students.



    All members must be CURRENT STUDENTS.
  • Where will I have to come to Register?

    The Registrations begin at 5:30PM in Vari Hall Rotunda. Eventually your teams will be allotted separate rooms within the same building.

  • Locations

  • Where will the competition take place?

    Registrations will take place in Vari Hall Rotunda, York University. After registrations team will be allotted separate rooms in Vari Hall. However you are free to go wherever you want as long as your team returns for the consultations and presentation.

  • Equipment

  • Do we pay extra for the equipment?

    Nope. We provide you the equipment you may need except for the laptops and your other personal tools. Further details will be released during the briefing.

  • Judging Criteria

  • Who are the Judges?

    We will be inviting a panel of experts representing different fields of the engineering industry to evaluate the performance.

  • What is the Judging Criteria?

    The criteria will be disclosed on the day of the event.

  • Food Options

  • Do I pay extra for the food?

    No, Food is covered in the registration fee. We will be providing supplies for
    March 11: Dinner + Snacks
    March 12: Breakfast + Lunch + Dinner + Snacks
    March 13: Optional day to finish your task (No meal provided)
    March 14: Breakfast

  • Food Restrictions

    If you have any specific Restrictions or Allergies, email us at contact@innovationbattleground.com, so we can make it right.

  • Sleeping Areas

  • Do we need to stay overnight?

    Completely upto you. According to the schedule each day ends at 11:00 PM but teams can choose to stay overnight. Rooms and snacks will be available.

  • Do we have a place to sleep?

    You have the option to relax in your allotted room.
